2. Place yourself into the center of your webcam

-- Now to control the snake --

a. Make a pyramid above your head to go UP

b. Make a pyramid below your chin to go DOWN

c. Raise your left hand visible to the webcam to go LEFT

d. Raise your right hand visible to the webcam to go RIGHT
